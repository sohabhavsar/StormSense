About StormSense

Welcome to StormSense — a digital weapon built for a cause that matters. Powered by raw code, weather data, and a deep passion for the people and their safety 🌍🔥 This project is the brainchild of a student with a mindset to learn more and help people in need. Yeah, I’m talking about me!!

With every storm we track, every temperature we display, we’re not just showing weather — we’re helping to prevent disaster. **Code with a purpose. Design with fire. That’s StormSense.**
